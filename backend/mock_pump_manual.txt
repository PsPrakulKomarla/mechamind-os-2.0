
INDUSTRIAL CENTRIFUGAL PUMP - MAINTENANCE MANUAL

1. INTRODUCTION
This document outlines the standard operating procedures and maintenance protocols for the X-200 Centrifugal Water Pump.

2. SAFETY PROTOCOLS
CRITICAL RISK: Before performing any maintenance, ensure Lockout/Tagout (LOTO) procedures are strictly followed.
WARNING: Do not bypass the main isolation valve.
If chemical leakage is detected near the primary seal, evacuate the immediate area and don Level B PPE before attempting to close the main isolation valve.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
Issue: Overheating
If the pump is overheating, bearing degradation is highly likely the cause. Please halt the machine immediately and inspect the front and rear bearings (OEM Manual Page 14).
If the bearings are damaged, replace them with OEM part #B-4092.

Issue: High Vibration
Check the impeller for cavitation or imbalance. Ensure the mounting bolts are tightened to 120 Nm.

4. PREVENTATIVE MAINTENANCE
- Monthly: Lubricate bearings with lithium-based grease.
- Bi-annually: Inspect mechanical seals for wear and tear. Replace if clearance exceeds 0.05mm.
